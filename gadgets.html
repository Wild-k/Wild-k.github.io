Edgar's 2 gadgets.

Edgar of Brawl Stars mythical brawler has over 2 gadgets. For his first gadget,Let's Fly
, drastically increases his Trait’s Super-charge for a short period of time, allowing Edgar
to load his Super quickly in order to jump to his enemies. His second Gadget, Hardcore,
provides him a shield that absorbs damage and decays over time. Hardcore gives an
extra 2000 hp. Both gadgets are equally strong.

Amber's 2 Gadgets!

Amber's 2 gadgets are as powerful as her normal attacks, including gadget no.1
, Fire Starters and gadget no.2, Dancing Flames. For Fire Starters, Amber moves
14% faster and leaves a trail of her fire fluid behind her for three seconds. The fire
fluid she spills behaves identically to that of her Super. Unlike her Super, using her Fire
Starters Gadget again doesn’t remove her
previous Gadget’s fluid. Next, for Dancing Flames, Amber spawns 3 fireballs that
revolve around her in a radius of 3.33 tiles, dealing 500 damage if they hit an enemy. Upon using the Gadget, all enemies within the area of effect instantly receive 500 damage and enemies who are on the same spot as Amber will take 1500 damage. Both of the gadgets are useful in all kinds of maps including Brawl Ball and Duo showdown.

Credits to Fandom!

Dynamike's 2 Gadgets

Dynamike's 2 gadgets are Fidget Spinner and Satchel Charge, since Dynamike's attacks are always throwers, this 2 gadgets will be good in maps with alot of walls. For Fidget Spinner, Dynamike rapidly fires a total of 20 sticks of dynamite within a 9 tile radius around him, each stick dealing 1200 damage upon detonation. He receives a 19% speed boost for 2 seconds, but is unable to attack while this Gadget is in effect. His Dyna-Jump Star Power(Later explained) doesn’t affect this Gadget. Secondly, for Satchel Charge, Dynamike's next attack stuns enemies hit by its explosion for 1.5 seconds. A Gadget symbol will glow above Dynamike's head signaling this Gadget's usage, as well as a glowing attack bomb. This Gadget's cooldown starts after this attack is shot.

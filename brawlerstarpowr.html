TOP BRAWLER STARPOWERS!

1. Edgar

Edgar has 2 Star Powers, Hard Landing and Fisticuffs.
1.Hard Landing, Upon landing from his Super,
Edgar deals 1000 damage in a 3-tile
radius around him. In addition, an altered landing indicator
shows the area-of-effect.

Credits to Fandom!

2.Amber

Amber has 2 Star Powers, Wild Flames and Scorchin' Siphon.
1.Wild Flames, Amber can have two fire fluid puddles from her Super
, and the first puddle only disappears if a third Super is used. Additionally,
when she’s standing near a puddle of fluid from either her Super or her
Fire Starters Gadget, her Super passively charges by 5% per second.
2. Scorchin Siphon', When Amber is near a puddle of fire fluid from
either her Super or Fire Starters Gadget, she reloads 50% faster and gains
a reddish appearance to indicate the Star Power is active. A minimum of 0.67
tiles into the puddle is required to activate the Star Power.

Credits to Fandom!

3. Dynamike

For Dyna-Jump, Dynamike's first Star Power. Dynamike's main attack
and Super can propel him into the air to jump a short distance opposite
and away from the detonation point. He can use this to jump over
obstacles and Brawlers. His Super propels him further than his attack
when detonated. While in the air, Dynamike is completely immune to all
damage besides status effects and damage applied over time. For Demolition
, Dynamike's Super deals 1000 more damage. This doesn’t affect other aspects of his Super.

Credits to Fandom!
